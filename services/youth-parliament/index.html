With this program we enable children to master all the basics and, a step further, we practically teach how a parliament works to bring prosperity to its people.

Often while hearing this word-parliament everybody will have thousands of running thoughts in their mind but all runs in the negative direction. Have you ever thought why do we fear?

We fear because from our childhood we all were squeezed between unorganized government and its ineffective actions. People are the supreme power of democratic country but they never used their potential to shape better government. The reason for this is, they are not aware of the mechanism as for how every government works. We have a little government running in our school, yes it is true we have this here! We have little Prime Minister, Home Minister and other notable cabinet ministers and we have respective subordinates for each minister. How does it help children? Simple if a child learns about how to emphasize a problem before another equivalent child in a positive way to get a suitable solution from their possessed knowledge. Through this, they learn the importance of obeying laws also they will know how to change a law or rule. The financial and environmental managing skills are inherited in their young ages. We are developing tomorrow’s great leaders so that our country soon grows as a developed nation than sustaining as developing nation.

By learning this, Parliament members are bringing out the problems faced by children for the sight of the headmaster and also they suggest solutions to the specific problem from a different perspective.
